Spot nutrition risk early, act with what you already have, and find real help near you.

NutriPath is a nutrition action platform for U.S. households, caregivers, and community workers facing food insecurity, low access, and rising diet-related health risk. It helps you decide what to do today, not just what to read.

Start Assessment Open Smart Meal Builder

1. Act Early on Nutrition Risk

Check warning signs before they get worse, including low appetite, weight loss, low activity, and growth concerns in children.

2. Build Better Meals From What You Already Have

Turn pantry and low-cost foods into practical meal options, see nutrient gaps, and get affordable foods to add next.

3. Find Real Support When Access Is Limited

Locate verified food and health resources, handle food desert constraints, and get clearer fallback options when nearby help is limited.

Built for real U.S. nutrition challenges

Families can face both hunger and poor diet quality at the same time. A full stomach is not always a nourished body. NutriPath supports children, older adults, and mixed households where obesity and nutrient deficiency can coexist.
