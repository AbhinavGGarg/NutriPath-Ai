AI Triage Local Meal Planning Offline-Ready PWA

Detect malnutrition early and deliver practical nutrition action in minutes.

NutriPath AI helps individuals, families, caregivers, health workers, and NGOs run screenings, generate low-cost local meal plans, and connect households to nearby support services from one web platform.

Start AI Assessment Open Program Dashboard

1. Guided Risk Screening

Capture MUAC, symptoms, diet diversity, and household context through a simple mobile-first form with low-literacy labels.

2. AI-Powered Action Plan

Get a risk score, urgent referral guidance, probable deficiencies, and a household nutrition plan using local affordable foods.

3. Resource Routing

Instantly find clinics, food support points, and NGO services with route-ready map cards and travel distance estimates.

Built for underserved communities

Interface includes icon-first controls, voice readouts, and fast loading on low bandwidth. Data can be captured in field visits and synced later.
